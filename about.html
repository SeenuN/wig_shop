About Wig Design Banashankari

At Wig Design Banashankari, we believe that hair is a powerful form of self-expression. For over a decade, we’ve specialized in crafting premium quality wigs that help people feel confident, stylish, and uniquely themselves.

Whether you're facing hair loss, experimenting with a new look, or preparing for a special event, our collection offers something for everyone — from natural-looking lace front wigs to bold fashion styles.

Our team of expert stylists and wig designers is passionate about helping clients choose the perfect piece. We use the highest quality human hair and synthetic fibers, ensuring durability and comfort in every design.

Visit us at our showroom in Bengaluru, India or shop online to explore the latest in wig trends and personalized consultations.
